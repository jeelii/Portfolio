More about me

#passions #journey #activities

I am Jenny Svensson, a Full-Stack developer in Malmö. I am passionate about usability and smart solutions. I love problem solving , which is why I love working with code. I am great at thinking outside the box and making things work.

I have a long experience as a designer/web developer building many custom websites. I have realized that I prefer developing features and coding over everything else in a project. I recently completed the Full-Stack JavaScript developer bootcamp at </Salt> and look forward to applying my new skills in the real world.

In my spare time I like to be quite active. I dance and DJ swing, work out, do yoga, am out and about in nature and doing things with friends. I want to contribute to a better world, so I try to minimize my carbon footprint by living minimalistically and eating a plant based diet. I also love experiencing new things and traveling.
