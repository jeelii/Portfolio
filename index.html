I am Jenny Lind, a Full Stack developer in Malmö. My passion lies in crafting user-friendly and innovative solutions. I thrive on problem-solving, which is why I'm drawn to coding. With a knack for thinking outside the box, I excel at finding creative ways to make things work seamlessly. My superpower is thinking outside the box.

My travels have taken me around the world, where I've had thrilling experiences like scuba diving in World War II shipwrecks, reaching depths of 40 meters.
